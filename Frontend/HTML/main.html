About Us

At Genedy Metal, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality aluminum doors, windows, kitchens, and more. With a focus on durability, modern design, and customer satisfaction, we deliver reliable solutions for homes and businesses across Egypt.

Excellence in Aluminum Manufacturing
